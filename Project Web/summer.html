Mountain tracking

Far away from civilization, located in a mountain range ladder is a phenomenal place for hiking in the mountains. Many trails leading through the local mountains and mountain huts allow you to spend many enjoyable days enjoying the fresh air and pristine peace.

Mountain biking

Every year Ladek hosts the greatest stars of the MB stage. In exciting competitions, the competitors compete for the title of the Polish Champion. Come and watch, and if you feel up to it, try the routes designed even for complete beginners. If you don't have your bike, don't be afraid. You can rent it on site.

Mushrooming!

Ladek, known throughout the region by its nature, is also an extremely fertile place for mushrooms. If you are passionate, combine your stay with mushroom picking, and you will certainly not regret it.
