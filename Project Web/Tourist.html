Ladek Zdroj Commune welcomes its visitors. On our website you will find information about entertainment and recreation opportunities in the area of our commune. Ladek Commune is the oldest health resort in Poland with a huge base of health and beauty treatments. We encourage you to familiarize yourself with the offer of our services.

Summer holidays

Winter holidays

Situated in a mountain range, the Ladek Zdroj Commune offers many different attractions for active recreation. In winter, the ski slopes and cross-country ski trails are waiting for adventure-loving skiers. In summer, visitors can admire the unique mountain scenery and take advantage of many cycling routes and many other attractions. Check the variety of our possibilities and you will not be disappointed!

Resort

The historical richness of our spa has overshadowed many places in the region and country. Being the oldest health resort in Poland, Ladek can boast 800 years of history together with many world-famous personalities who have benefited from the services of our health resort. Ladek offers a rich database of health and regeneration services and beauty services. Ladek offers treatments in close proximity to several health houses located close to each other. Relaxation in the resort is accompanied by beautiful park buildings, peace and quiet. An ideal place to relax from the city.
